Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

1. Introduction

Dengzhou Deyuanbao Technology Co., Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.dzdeyuanbao.com (the "Site") or use our mobile applications (the "Apps") available on Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our Site or Apps, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact us for support
  • Request business information
  • Participate in surveys or promotions

This information may include: name, email address, phone number, postal address, company name, job title, and other contact information.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our Site or Apps, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Device identifiers
  • Referring website or application
  • Pages viewed and time spent on pages
  • Date and time of access
  • Geographic location (country/region level)

2.3 Mobile Application Information

Our mobile applications may collect additional information including:

  • Device name and model
  • Unique device identifiers (UDID, IMEI, IDFA)
  • Mobile advertising identifiers (GAID, IDFA)
  • Network information (WiFi, cellular)
  • Installed applications list
  • Storage usage
  • Battery level and status

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • Personalize and improve your experience
  • Deliver targeted advertising and marketing communications
  • Comply with legal obligations

We use third-party advertising and analytics services to display advertisements within our Apps and on our Site. These services may collect information about your visits to our Site and Apps and your online activities over time and across different websites and applications.

4.3 Types of Advertisements

Our Apps may display the following types of advertisements:

  • Splash Ads (App Open Ads): Full-screen advertisements displayed when opening or closing the application
  • Rewarded Video Ads: Video advertisements that users can watch to receive in-app rewards
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen advertisements displayed during natural transition points in the app
  • Banner Ads: Rectangular advertisements displayed at the top or bottom of the screen
  • Native Ads: Advertisements designed to match the look and feel of the app content
  • Playable Ads: Interactive advertisements that allow users to try a game before downloading

4.4 Analytics Services

We use analytics services to understand how users interact with our Apps, including:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Adjust
  • AppsFlyer
  • Branch
  • Firebase Crashlytics

5. Types of Data Collected by Ad Platforms

Advertising and analytics platforms may collect the following types of data:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, GAID, UDID, IMEI)
  • Device characteristics (manufacturer, model, operating system)
  • Device settings (language, time zone, screen size)
  • Network information (carrier, connection type)
  • App usage data and interaction patterns
  • Ad interaction history
  • Approximate geographic location
  • Advertising campaign performance data

6. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: You have given clear consent for us to process your personal data for specific purposes
  • Contract: Processing is necessary for a contract we have with you
  • Legal Obligation: Processing is necessary for us to comply with the law
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing is necessary for our legitimate interests, provided those interests are not overridden by your rights

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

8. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information collected
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: You may opt out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@dzdeyuanbao.com

9. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Our Apps and services are not directed to children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Age Restrictions

Our Apps are intended for users who are:

  • 13 years of age or older (for US-based services under COPPA)
  • 16 years of age or older (for EEA-based services under GDPR)
  • Of the age of digital consent in their respective countries

We reserve the right to restrict access to certain features or content based on age verification.

12. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including:

  • Providing our services
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Resolving disputes
  • Enforcing our agreements

Advertising identifiers and related data collected by third-party ad platforms are subject to their respective retention policies.

13. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. When we transfer your data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Binding Corporate Rules
  • Privacy Shield certification (where applicable)
  • Other legally approved transfer mechanisms

Our Site and Apps may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites or services you visit.

16. Your Choices

16.1 Mobile App Choices

You can control certain data collection and advertising preferences through your device settings:

  • iOS Devices: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android Devices: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

16.2 Web Browser Choices

You can opt out of certain advertising and analytics by:

  • Enabling "Do Not Track" settings in your browser
  • Using browser extensions that block tracking
  • Clearing cookies regularly
  • Using private/incognito browsing modes

16.3 Analytics Opt-Out

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
