Extended FAQ

More answers to common questions

How can I become a partner?

We welcome partnerships in technology development, distribution, and regional representation. Contact our business development team for partnership proposals and opportunities.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, PayPal, and various payment platforms. For bulk orders, flexible payment terms are available upon discussion.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our products. Installation costs vary by product type and location. Contact our sales team for details.

What warranty do you provide?

All products come with a standard 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranties are available for purchase. Terms vary by product category.

Can I visit your office?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors to our office. Please schedule an appointment at least 48 hours in advance through our contact form or by emailing our reception.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Contact us for specific shipping quotations to your location.
